Tacos

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand. A taco can be made with a variety of fillings, including beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans, vegetables, and cheese, allowing for great versatility and variety. A taco is generally eaten without utensils and is often accompanied by garnishes such as salsa, avocado, guacamole, cilantro, tomatoes, onions, and lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Protein (beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans)
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, onions)
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas
  2. Cook protein
  3. Assemble tacos with protein, cheese, vegetables, and garnishes
  4. Enjoy!
